Quickpress allows you to create, delete and list your posts and pages on the command line.

With two to three commands, you're all set for managing you Wordpress blog.

Post quickly

Write the post on your favorite text editor and let quickpress take care of the rest.

Write the way you like

Quickpress supports several templating languages: Markdown, Asciidoc, Textile, Haml, ERB and much more!

Manage several sites at once.

Switch between Wordpress sites easily and post on all of them.

Actions

With quickpress you'll be able to

  • Create, Delete, Edit posts and pages.
  • List and create categories.
  • Check status on comments and users.
  • See settings on the Wordpress site.

And more, with straightforward commands.

features you'll love

Quickpress is:

  • Powerful: With quickpress you can create, delete and list your posts and pages. See actions on the left.
  • Easy-to-use: Two to three commands should be everything you need to know about it.
  • Versatile: It supports a wide range of templating languages. Markdown, Asciidoc, ERB and much more!
  • Documented: The wiki has all the guides you'll ever need. Also, reference is only a qp help away.
  • Unified: Works on both Wordpress.com and self-hosted Wordpress.org websites.
  • Safe: Quickpress doesn't store your username/password anywhere. It also supports three different forms of authentication - choose your favorite.
  • Free: It is licensed under the GPLv3. It means that quickpress will always be free
    (both as in "free beer" and as in "freedom").

download and install

quickpress is available on RubyGems.

The preferred method to install is with rubygems:

$ gem install quickpress

If you want to install it yourself, grab the .gem file here.
